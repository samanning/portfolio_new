Good Oats

Good Oats is a conceptual brand and product made for people on the go.

Created the identity for a conceptual brand, Good Oats, and designed product packaging.

Process

Take a look at my design process, from creating a logo to building a package prototype.

The Challenge

Design a cohesive brand identity for a food product and create packaging that is sustainable, yet based on human centered needs.

The Solution

A simplistic, yet meaningful logo and brand identity that can be applied to many mediums. Also, a sustainable package that is easy to carry, compostable, and insulated. This prototype demonstrates what the exterior and interior of the packaging. The true package will be made of a sustainable mushroom material that decomposes in soil. This mushroom material is ideal for the environment because it is renewable, natural, replenishes soil with rich nutrients when it decomposes.
