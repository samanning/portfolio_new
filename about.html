Hi there, I'm Samantha, but most just call me Sam. I’m a multidisciplinary designer that specializes in web design and development. I recently graduated from the University of California, Davis and received a BA in Design and a BA in Communication.

In my design work, I’m passionate about creating experiences. My design aestethic tends to be minimalistic, clean, yet functional. I enjoy learning about and practicing human centered design. Aside from design, I love food, travelling, and watching old Beyoncé concerts online. When I’m not coding or designing anything, you can find me sipping on a cup of coffee or watching a Golden State Warriors game.
