Hi, I'm Sam.

I’m a multidisciplinary designer interested in UI & UX, design thinking, and human centered design. In 2016, I graduated from UC Davis and received a B.A. in Design and a B.A. in Communication. Recently, I furthered my education by taking an intensive User Experience Design course at General Assembly, which I completed in March 2019.

In my design work, I’m passionate about creating experiences. My design aesthetic tends to be minimalistic, clean, yet functional. I enjoy learning about and practicing human centered design. Aside from design I love food, traveling, and vibing out to old Beyoncé concert videos. When I’m not designing, you can find me sipping on cups of coffee or watching a Golden State Warriors game.
