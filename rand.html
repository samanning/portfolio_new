Design and the Play Instinct

Created the layout for Paul Rand's book, Design and the Play Instinct, and bound the book by hand.

Construction

To bind the book pages, I used the perfect binding method. Since I did not have a book press, I made my own out of two bamboo cutting boards and screws. My DIY book press tightly held the square pages for my book, which made it very easy to apply glue to the book spine edge.

The Challenge

Develop and utilize a grid system to layout the copy and images from the original publication. Limited to only use black and white, plus one spot color.

The Solution

A hand bound book with a simple, yet playful layout.
