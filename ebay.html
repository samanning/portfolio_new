eBay

eBay is where the world goes to shop, sell, and give. Our mission is to be the world's favorite destination for discovering great value and unique selection. As a Visual Designer for eBay's North American Creative team, I develop, design, and produce on-brand assets and pages for eBay's website.

eBay Deals

Deals assets are targeted to promote great product selection, the best bargains, and free shipping to help consumers shop with confidence. I design campaigns for "Daily Deals" and deals during special retail moments like Black Friday, Labor Day, and Spring Black Friday. The assets below are some of the highest performers and GMV drivers in the Deals vertical, beating click-through rate benchmarks by 0.5-1.2%.

Authorized Resellers

I partnered with brands such as Fender, PRS, Taylor Guitars, and more to promote that eBay is the one stop shop for authentic musical instruments and gear at everyday low prices. As the lead designer, I conceptualized and developed onsite assets and a landing page to showcase numerous authentic brands on our platform.

Under $10

The Under $10 campaign was created to be a curated marketplace for millenials to shop bargains and novelties, all priced under $10. Compared to other eBay landing pages, Under $10 is a more curated, easily browsable destination with a unique design. We intentionally designed Under $10 assets to look more playful than typical "business-as-usual" eBay campaigns.
