Davis Bike Loop

A conceptual wayfinding system and rebrand for the Davis Bike Loop, a 12 mile bike path around the city of Davis, CA. The goal of the project is to make it easier for people to navigate through the city and provide an interactive experience for commuters and visitors.

Process

Below, check out our in-depth process from inital inspiration moodboards to final in-situ renderings.

My Role

Co-Designer and Researcher

The Challenge

Developing an interactive and engaging wayfinding system for bike riders. Finding a way to create a greater sense of community around the bike loop. Making the bike loop easy to navigate, yet still interactive.

The Solution

A monumental style wayfinding system that showcases Davis' vast agricultural history and directs people along the bike route.
