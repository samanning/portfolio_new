Hummingbird Health and Conservation Program

Hummingbird Health and Conservation Program is a group of researchers interested in protecting and promoting hummingbird biology, health, disease ecology and conservation. The group looked to create promotional pieces to give to the public and their volunteers. We designed an identity and promotional items for the group to give them a stronger presence in the Davis community.

Informational Postcard

The postcard (below) was designed to inform the public about what the Hummingbird Health and Conservation Program does and how people can get involved with the group. You may find these postcards at the Davis Farmer's Market in Davis, CA.

Blank Notecards

These notecards feature hummingbird species native to Northern California. They were designed as promotional gifts for volunteers of the HHCP. Notecards come in a package of six cards with a blank message inside. Below is an example of one notecard that features a Costa's hummingbird.

The Challenge

Using a grid to layout the most important information on the postcard.

The Solution

A straight-forward, yet pleasing to look at postcard that is educational and has a call to action. Also, a beautiful series of notecards that bring awareness to a nonprofit group and are perfect for gifts.
