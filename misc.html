Miscellaneous Works

Featured below are other design projects I have worked on. These projects range from motion graphics, environmental graphics, UI/UX exercises, and textiles.

Lift Off Music Video

Designed and developed a lyric music video for Jay-Z and Kanye West's song Lift Off, featuring Beyonce. Used different typography to emphasize two distinct voices and explored creating motion graphics with AfterEffects.

Stephen Curry Animation Exercise

Created a rustic frame by frame animation of Stephen Curry's infamous step-back three point shot.

Design Your Own Terrarium

Used jQuery to build an interface that lets you design your own terrarium by dragging and dropping elements into a terrarium bowl. Create your own terrarium here!

Museum of Wonder and Delight Wayfinding Signage

Developed signage for a whimsical museum in Folsom, California called the Museum of Wonder and Delight. Created small signage throughout the entire musuem and created special signs out of paper bags for an exhibit exclusively about shopping bags. See some of the signange in the video below.

Japanese Noh Textile & Pillow

Created a hand printed textile on yards of cotton sateen fabric and sewed a pillow with the textile. This geometric pattern was inspired by Japanese Noh theater costumes.

Silk Screen Textiles

Collection of silk screen textiles - a floral, geometric, and novelty print.

Type Hierarchy Studies

Type posters for a hypothetical lecture series concerning design, product design, and architecture. Explored how to make engaging compositions by incorporating organizational and visual principles such as rhythm, texture, contrast, proximity, and grid structures.
